One counter, two reputations

Famous for brunch. Loved for the Indian food.

Only U is a family-run campus spot known for its all-day brunch — and, just as much, for some of the most authentic Indian food on the west side. Regulars come for the eggs benny and stay for the butter chicken. Order whichever you're craving.

The Diner

Brunch, done properly

Cooked to order, served all day, exactly how a campus classic should be.

About Only U

Good food. Good people. Right on campus.

For years, Only U Cafe has fed the UBC community from its corner of the Old Marketplace in UBC Village — students between classes, faculty on a break, neighbours who've been coming for a decade. Generous portions, fair prices, and a kitchen that's always happy to customize. Come as you are.
